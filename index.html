How Pawdia works

Not a directory.
A scoring system.

Most cafes allow dogs on their terrace but never say so. Traditional directories only list pet cafes — missing 90% of places that'd genuinely welcome your dog.

01
📡
We scan every place

Every cafe, restaurant, bar, hotel, park and groomer gets evaluated — not just places that call themselves pet-friendly. If it has outdoor seating or allows pets, it gets a score.

02
🎯
We score honestly

A cafe that tolerates dogs outside gets a 6. A place with a dog menu, water bowls and staff who love it gets a 9 or 10. No inflated scores.

03
📋
You see the exact policy

Every place shows the specific policy — "outdoor only, large breeds fine" or "dogs inside, no restrictions." You know before you leave home.

04
❤️
Community keeps it honest

42,000 dog owners on @oscar_and_mojo contribute real reviews with actual breeds and experiences. Scores stay accurate over time.

9–10
Dogs are central — menu, water bowls, welcome inside, staff who love it
7–8
Dogs clearly welcome outdoors, water on request, warm and friendly staff
5–6
Dogs tolerated on terrace — no amenities but useful to know about
0–4
